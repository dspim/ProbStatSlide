範例

  • 我們賣出\(n\)個產品，\(X\)代表總共需要維修的產品數量，\(Y = g(X) = n - X\)是\(X\)的函數
  • 假設\(X\)的PMF如下：

\[f_X(x) = P(X = x) = \left(\begin{array}{c}n \\ x\end{array}\right)p^x (1-p)^{n - x}, x = 0, 1, ..., n\]

  • 求\(Y\)的PMF：設定\(g^{-1}(y)\)是一個集合，代表所有\(x\)使得\(g(x) = y\)，則：

\[f_Y(y) = \sum_{x \in g^{-1}(y)} f_X(x) = f_X(n - y) = \left(\begin{array}{c}n \\ y\end{array}\right)(1-p)^yp^{n-y}\]

  • 等到之後介紹常見的機率分布時，同學會發現這裡的\(X\)是\(Binomial(n, p)\)，\(Y\)是\(Binomial(n, 1 - p)\)

\[\begin{array}{rcll} f_Y(y) & = & \sum_{x \in g^{-1}(y)} f_X(x) & \\ & = & f_X(n - y) & \\ & = & \left(\begin{array}{c} n \\ n - y\end{array}\right)p^{n-y}(1 - p)^{n - (n-y)} & = \left(\begin{array}{c} n \\ y\end{array}\right)(1-p)^{y}p^{n-y} \end{array}\]
